# ShohojKhata Product Definition & Guidelines

ShohojKhata is a hyper-local, multi-tenant digital ledger marketplace designed for small business owners, local merchants, and daily retail clients. It acts as a "Digital Mall for Khata Books" where instead of tracking running accounts (Udhari Hisab) in physical paper notebooks, merchants run a digital storefront on ShohojKhata. Clients can browse the platform, find their local shops, join them, and view their live running bills in real time.

## Core User Experience & Workflows

### 1. Zero-Friction Signup
- **Google Authentication**: Users sign up instantly using Google Login.
- **Fast Secure Access**: After signup, users configure their phone number and set a 4-digit PIN in the Settings Menu for fast, secure access.

### 2. The Premium Merchant Gate
- **Store Creation**: Shopkeepers unlock merchant tools by participating in the Premium Store tier.
- **Store Directory**: Active store details (Shop Name, Category, Location) are published live to the public directory.

### 3. The Marketplace Connection
- **Shop Section Discovery**: Clients browse the public marketplace listing active local businesses, find their regular retailer, and tap "Join Shop" to link their profile instantly.

### 4. Live Ledger Updates
- **Daily Bookkeeping**: The merchant writes transactions in real time (e.g., "Akash: 50 ada, 50 cha, 50 gorom moshla — 150 rs due").
- **Real-Time Client Dashboard**: Clients immediately see matching itemized entries on their devices.
